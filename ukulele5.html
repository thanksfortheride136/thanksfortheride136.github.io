Ukulele Assembly Instructions


Step 5.
In this step we are gluing in the internal frames on both the ukulele bottom and top. This is an important step. It must be aligned on the INSIDE of the lines, see video for reference. This make take some adjusting to get the frames perfect, they are very flexible. You also must but wood glue all around the frame, dont miss any spots!
