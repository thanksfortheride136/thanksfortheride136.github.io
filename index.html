Welcome to Technology

Mr. Cooper

The Technology program at Tuckahoe Middle & High school aims to create adaptable, technically proficient learners through several different technology domains. Students have the option to choose from several different electives in high school, students also take STEAM in middle school to get an idea for what technology concepts they may have interest in. Technology Education is a crucial part of learning in a modern 21st century school, while students are not all aiming to be technology professionals in the future, students can use the skillsets learned in these classes in other areas of education and work. In the technology room students will have access to industry standard 3-D modeling software, Adobe Suite, hand tools, several 3-D printers, A lasercutter, vacuum former, soldering stations, Arduino & Raspberry Pi's. Chemistry in the Community will also be taught in the Technology room this year by Mr. Cooper.
