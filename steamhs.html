High School STEAM

Room 263G

In High School students in the STEAM elective will explore a variety of technical topics to gain knowledge on several different technological domains. The Engineering Design Process is the bedrock for the STEAM class. Students will start to learn and contextualize how the world around them is designed. Students will complete several real world design projcts that encourage teamwork, critical thinking and engage students in deep project based learning. In the technology classroom students have access to 3-D printers, a lasercutter, handtools, a vacuum former, vinyl cutter, and several pieces of industry standard software. STEAM is a fantastic choice for any student interested in learning about 3-D modeling, design, engineering, product design, industrial design, art and any student with a deep interest in technology. Students of all levels are welcome in class, I love to see students growth, by the end of the class all students will have gone from ideas in their head to physical products.
