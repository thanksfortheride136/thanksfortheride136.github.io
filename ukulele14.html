Ukulele Assembly Instructions


Step 14.
In this step we are finally gluing the fretboard, front plate of the headstock, as well as the saddle and nut to the ukulele. First we are going to hammer the saddle into the bridge. Then we glue the front plate of the hearstock, this allows us to take the nut and use the edge of the headstock to align the nut. Ensure the non-flat side of the nut is "flush" with the front headstock plate. After this place woodglue on the fretboard, align to the flat side of the neck body. Remember to clamp.
