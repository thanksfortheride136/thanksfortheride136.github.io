Ukulele Assembly Instructions


Step 13.
Installing the fret wire onto the ukulele fretboard can be a challenge. We are aligning the fret wire to each groove and then cutting roughly the correct size (leave overhang). You then will put glue onto the wires and then hammer into the grooves in the fretboard. Please wear safety glasses when cutting the fretwire, the wires tend to fly all over.
