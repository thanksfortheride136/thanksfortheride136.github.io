Engineering

Room 263G

The High School Engineering Class provides students with a foundational understanding of the vast field of engineering. Through hands-on projects, collaborative teamwork, and real-world applications, students will explore various engineering disciplines including civil, mechanical, electrical, and chemical engineering. They will be introduced to the principles of design, problem-solving methodologies, and the role of engineering in society
