Ukulele Assembly Instructions


Step 2.
This step involves gluing the ribs of the ukulele together. These ribs play a vital role in the strength of our ukulele. The ribs stpan across the body plate and prevent warping from the strength of the string tension. The ribs also allow the ukulele to vibrate at certain frequencies and make the sound we are so used to.
