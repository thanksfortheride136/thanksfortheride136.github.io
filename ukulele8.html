Ukulele Assembly Instructions


Step 8.
We will assemble our glued faces onto each other. This part requires you to assemble the "body block" and the "neck assembly" in order to join the faces together. The bigger more round side of the ukulele takes the body block assembly and the smaller side takes the neck assembly. No gluing is required of the body block or neck, you do need to glue the holes they are inserting into. Look at the orientation of how you place the body block and neck assembly. If you put any of these upsided down you will need to start this entire project over.
