Ukulele Assembly Instructions


Step 7.
This is the neck assembly, it is a complicated assembly that requires you to think on the fly and be flexible in your clamping and assembly. You will glue the center ribs into the headstock first, you will then align the sides into the headstock and the top part of the neck. You may need a hammer for this.
