Media Production

Room 263G

Media Production class focuses on student expression through a variety of artistic mediums. Students will create art, interactive epxeriences, designs, physical items to express themselves. Students will utilize several exciting pieces of software in order to complete projects, through these projects students will gain an understanding and appreciation for media in all its forms. Students will be creating choose your own adventure stories, online portfolios using HTML / CSS, lasercut card and album cover projects, exploring the role of Artificial Intelligence in Art and our lives, learning about video editing, graphic design utilizing Adobe Photoshop and illustrator and much more. All students are welcome regardless of artistic skill, media is for everyone and we will all create beautiful projects in this class together. Students who need extra help can schedule it using the button below. I always welcome students for extra help!
