Ukulele Assembly Instructions


Step 10.
You are now gluing the body sides to the body. You need a partner for this part, it is very difficult to do by yourself. We are utilizing a jig. In engineering a "jig" is a part that is used to create another part. So in this case we are using a jig to push together the ukulele sides in an easy manner. Without this tool it would not be possible to put enough pressure on all sides at the same time to do this.
