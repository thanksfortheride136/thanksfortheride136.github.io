Chemistry in the Community

Room 263G

Chemistry in the Community explores how chemistry effects every day life. An applied Chemistry class with lab, this class will give students a greater understanding for "why" Chemistry is an integral part of science and our lives. Students will have the opportunity to explore hands on projects that are designed to show students Chemistry, rather than just conceptualize it.
