Middle School STEAM

Room 263G

Middle school STEAM focuses on exposing our students to as much new technology concepts as we can. Students will explore several different technology concepts in: Digital Literacy, Computer Science, Engineering, 3-D Modeling, 3-D Printing, Lasercutting, Design, Email etiquette, Typing, problem solving and much more. The goal of the middle school STEAM program is to allow students to explore their varied interest in technology and prepare them for the slate of high school electives as well as give them invaluable technological and critical thinking skills that can be used in school and other parts of their lives. Technology is not just about computers, technology are tools we create to solve human problems. With this framing we examine technology, its impacts and how our world is shaped by it. Students will leave STEAM with an appreciation for the world around them and perhaps a newfound interest / greater appreciation for technology.
