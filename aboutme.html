Bio

I am a passionate Technology Education / Engineering / Computer Science Teacher. I have worked for 8 years in public education teaching a wide variety of technological subjects to children. I have worked in Engineering and Architecture, taking projects from conception to completion. My passions include incorporating real world applications in the class, justifying the hard work that my students undertake. I specialize in project based learning (PBL) and a wide variety of technological skills. I currently teach classes in: Computer science, web development, 3-D printing / 3-D modeling, lasercutting, graphic design, engineering and media arts.
